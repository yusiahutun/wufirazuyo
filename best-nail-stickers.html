Nail Sticker FAQs

Do I need nail glue to apply nail stickers?

Most of the best nail stickers feature an adhesive side that easily sticks to your polish or bare nail without the need of glue.

How long do these stickers last?

There's no definitive time frame for nail stickers, but they can last up to a week or more with proper maintenance and care. Applying a coat or two of clear polish can help protect them against everyday wear and tear.

Are nail stickers difficult to remove?

Most stickers should be easily removed with the help of your go-to polish remover or peeled away with no damage to the nail bed.
