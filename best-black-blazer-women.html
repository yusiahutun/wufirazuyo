Best Black Blazer with a Backstory

Dior 30 Montaigne Bar Jacket

Best Black Blazer with a Backstory

Dior 30 Montaigne Bar Jacket

Now 48% Off

Credit: Dior

Dior's bar jacket is a piece of fashion history and the most elevated form of a black blazer a woman could own. This jacket with a nipped-in waist and fabric-covered buttons was first introduced in 1947 as part of Christian Dior's "New Look"; in the present, Maria Grazia Chiuri has refined it for colder weather in a warm, wool knit. In addition to frequent sightings at Paris fashion week, the bar jacket also appeared on the BAZAAR 100 in 2021, and is still a favorite for our best-dressed editors. If a blazer you want to pass down for generations is your priority, this is the one to invest in.

Sizes34–46
ColorBlack
Materials100% Wool
