New Moon in Gemini Will Affect Every Zodiac Sign 2023

Summer is right around the corner, and everyone’s booked and busy! Leave it to a Mercury-ruled season to get the ball rolling with calendar invites and general miscellanies, but if you’re wondering how the new moon in Gemini will affect each zodiac sign during June 2023, be sure to keep your eyes peeled as there will be an influx of information to consider moving forward. Mercury is also buzzing through its sign of rulership, which makes things all the more fast-paced and mentally stimulating.

Our friendly neighborhood Gemini is the first air sign in the zodiac. Swift and savvy, its mutable modality speaks to this air sign’s mental agility, and sparkling intellect. When considering the divine order of the zodiac wheel, it’s after we get in touch with our five senses (during Taurus season) that we’re ready to experience our surroundings with a newly found perspective, which is where Gemini season comes into play. For reference, check which astrological house belongs to this mutable air sign in your birth chart. This is where you’re more likely to indulge your curiosities, or simply overthink things more than you should. On a brighter note, this is the area of life where you’re likely most comfortable harnessing your duality, and/or have a way with words.

Now let’s take a closer look at the astrology of this month’s new moon, as well as the significance of this lunar phase. Deeply mysterious and often overlooked by its lack of visibility, the new moon is equally as fertile as it is supercharged, considering it also marks the beginning of a brand-new lunar cycle. This occurs when the sun joins forces with the moon via the same degree of a zodiac sign. Let’s not forget the moon represents our emotional foundation, while the sun symbolizes our ego and physical identity. The divine synergy of the two luminaries is precisely what makes this lunar phase so incredibly potent.

The new moon in Gemini takes place on June 18 at 12:32 a.m. ET

This year’s new moon in Gemini will take place on June 18 at exactly 12:32 a.m. ET. Renewing itself via 26 degrees of this curious air sign, it’s important to point out the pattern we’ve been seeing with the past new moons, as they’ve repeatedly activated the later degrees of their prospective signs. And though there’s an air of finality to these astrological degrees, it’s simultaneously bringing awareness to what needs to be closed out of our lives. For instance, when considering Gemini’s archetype, this could revolve around a mindset you’re no longer resonating with, or perhaps a familiar energy within your immediate surroundings.

Either way, you’re gaining insight and likely seeing both sides of a situation, which is helpful for those who are in the midst of change. New information is headed our way, and with a new-found perspective, we will have the clarity needed to make the necessary adjustments. On a fuzzier note, this lunation will be at odds with ambiguous Neptune, which is where the energy could potentially trigger feelings of restlessness and/or uncertainty. In other words, getting the answers you’ve been seeking could bring up more questions, and doubts. However, this is where we’re being encouraged to be still and surrender the need to overanalyze.

Sounds contradicting when considering Gemini’s cerebral nature, but if there’s ever been a good time to harness the duality of this air sign, it’s right now. And though your intuitive and logical mind are bound to challenge one another at this time, it’s up to you to find a healthy balance between the two. Let your thoughts simmer, but don’t overlook what you intuitively know to be true.
