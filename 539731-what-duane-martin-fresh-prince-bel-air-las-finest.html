Early life

Duane Martin was born on August 11, 1965, in Brooklyn, New York, United States. He grew up in New York alongside his siblings Michelle, Michael, and Vernell. He attended New York University. Before graduating, he briefly played NCAA Division III basketball at NYU.

After graduating from university, Duane tried his luck in the late 1980s to play basketball. Unfortunately, he never played the game professionally and decided to become an actor.

Professional career

When he left NYU, Duane Martin was signed as an undrafted free agent to a non-guaranteed contract in the National Basketball Association with the New York Knicks in 1989. However, the team cut him out and never played in the NBA. As a result, Duane transitioned to acting.

His first appearance in a TV series was in 1991 in Against the Law. He then appeared as Vidal Thomas in the NBC comedy series Out All Night. Also, Martin was featured in the Boyz II Men music video for I'll Make Love to You in 1994.

Wife and children

The L.A's Finest star married Tisha Campbell on August 17, 1996. The couple had met while teenagers on the set of an after-school movie about teen pregnancy. Tisha Campbell is an American actress born on October 13, 1968, in Oklahoma, United States.

Tisha Campbell and Duane Martin welcomed two sons, Xen Martin (born in 2001) and Ezekiel Czar Martin (born in 2009). In February 2018, Tisha filed for divorce after 21 years of marriage. They finalised their divorce in December 2020.

After the divorce, the American actor started dating actress and model Ashley Marie Jones. According to People, in February 2024, Duane Martin proposed to Ashley Marie Jones. He lives in California, United States.

Duane Martin's bankruptcy and debts

According to Daily Mail Online, he filed for Chapter 7 bankruptcy in 2016. In the filing, the actor and his wife claimed to be drowning in $15 million worth of debt. Furthermore, the documents stated that he only had $313,000 in assets.

Their listed assets include $65,000 in real estate and $248,000 in personal property. Their combined monthly income was $7,655, but their monthly expenses were $16,953.

The filing showed they owned a 2014 Range Rover, a 2016 Range Rover, a 1999 Kawasaki Drifter, and a Jeep Wrangler. In 2004, Tisha and Duane paid $880,000 for a Lake Arrowhead, California vacation home. They sold the house in 2009 for $1.597 million.

In 2005, they paid $900,000 for a 9,000-square-foot home in Chatsworth, California, which became their primary residence for the next 13 years. Around the time of their 2018 divorce filing, the couple listed the property for sale for $2.695 million.
