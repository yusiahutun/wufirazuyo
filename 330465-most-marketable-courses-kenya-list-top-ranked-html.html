35 most marketable courses in Kenya 2024: top-ranked degrees

In Kenya, one has numerous options to consider after leaving high school. These include pursuing university studies and joining a college or a technical institution. Whatever the case, the course one chooses can determine how easily one gets job opportunities after graduating. While it's advisable to consider one's passion, a course's marketability is equally essential. What are the most marketable courses in Kenya?

Mechanical engineering may be one of the oldest branches of engineering, but it remains a marketable course in Kenya. It deals with the study of physical machines that utilise force and motion.

Why it’s marketable: Degree holders in mechanical engineering are not only restricted to hands-on duties. They can also learn new technologies, up their project management skills, and become administrators or managers.
