What is an Olive Skin Tone?

Olive skin tones have a stunning golden glow, something very similar to what you would get after spending a sunny day at the beach – it's quite warm and with hints of green or yellow. This skin tone is most commonly seen in individuals who are from the Mediterranean, Middle East, or those from certain Asian backgrounds. If you're blessed enough to have olive skin, earthy tones, and warm hues are the ideal lip shades for you to look your best.

The following is a mini guide to help you understand undertones better:

Cool Undertones: They have red, blue, or pinkish tones

Warm Undertones: They feature orangish, peachy, honeyed yellowed, or golden tones

Neutral Undertones: This is a mixture of both cool and warm undertones

    How to Choose the Best Lip Color for Olive Skin

    Here's a step-by-step guide to help you select the most flattering lip colors for olive skin tones:

    Undertone: Firstly, determine your skin undertone. People with olive skin usually have warm or cool undertones. So, identify whether your skin has more green or yellow undertones, as this will guide your lip color choices.

    Shade Selection: Once you know which undertone you have, it will be easier for you to select the right lipstick colors. For example, if you have warm undertones, opt for lip colors with warm undertones as well, such as shades with hints of peach, coral, or red.

    On the contrary, if you have cool undertones, look for lip colors with cool tones, such as berry tones or blue-based reds.

    For people who have olive skin and feel they should avoid pale lip colors, our contributor Maya Adivi, a certified makeup artist, suggests, “It is not necessary to avoid pale lip colors, although it might be hard to find a flattering match. Contrary to popular belief, olive skin tone doesn't mean "tanned skin". It's actually a skin tone with a greenish tint. If you have fair, olive skin you could look great in a pale lipstick as long as it flatters your undertones.”

    Depth:  The depth of your skin tone matters when choosing lip colors. For instance, lighter olive skin tones can choose softer shades like light pinks, rosy nudes, or peachy pinks, and peachy tones. Medium to deep olive skin tones can explore bolder options like rich berries, mauves, or deep reds.

    How to Use Lip Color for Olive Skin

    To make the most of the best lip color for olive skin, here are some makeup tips on how to use it effectively:

    • Step 1: Start by prepping your lips. Exfoliate them gently to remove any dry or flaky skin. This can be done by using a lip scrub or a soft toothbrush to achieve a smooth canvas. Then, follow up with a hydrating lip balm to moisturize your lips and ensure a smooth application.
    • Step 2: Once your lips are all scrubbed and exfoliated, select a lip liner to define and shape your lips while also preventing your lip color from feathering or bleeding. Choose a liner that will match your chosen color or a shade that is similar to your natural lip color. Then, outline your lips and fill them in slightly with the liner to create a base.
    • Step 3: Now, apply the lip color. You can use a brush or directly put it on your lips. 
    • Step 4: Since olive skin tones often have depth and complexity, you can try to be creative and play with color combinations. You can experiment with layering different shades or mixing lip colors to achieve a custom look. For example, adding a touch of a lighter or darker shade to your chosen lip color can create a unique effect that complements your olive skin tone.
    • Step 5: Blot, set, and you are done!

    FAQ's

    What lip color is best for olive skin?

    All kinds of lip colors suit olive skin. If you want a subtle hint of pink, you can try your hands on nude pink shades and if you want something more profound, then try terracotta or earthy brown shades.

    What is the best makeup color for olive skin tone?

    The best makeup colors for olive skin tone could be shades of gold, bronze, and copper as highlighters and eyeshadow. The best lipstick could be nude with pink tints.

    How do I find my perfect lip color?

    You can find your perfect lip color by determining your skin color and undertone. Or you can just buy some shades and try to be creative by mixing and matching.

    What is the most seductive lip color?

    Red has been known to be the sexiest lip color of all time.

    What color lipstick makes you look younger?

    Both bold and subtle colors make you look younger. However, not all colors may go well with your skin color. You just have to them before selecting the right one.

    What color lipstick should you avoid if you have an olive skin tone?

    If you have an olive skin tone, we suggest that you avoid lipsticks that are overly cool-toned. Stay away from very light pinks or blues as they can clash with the warmth that your skin tone emits.

    Which lip color is the most attractive on olive skin?

    Warm tones such as terracotta, coral, or rich reds may look very attractive on people with olive skin tones.
